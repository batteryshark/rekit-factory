## Goal
Turn strict outbox, preference, and transport contracts into a restart-safe sender without making delivery authoritative over Factory state.

## Requirements
- [ ] Persist exact preference identities, schedules, channel references, and delivery dispositions outside run-authored payloads
- [ ] Supervise due immediate/batch/digest/escalation work with bounded leases, retry, and external idempotency
- [ ] Provide an environment-owned local desktop implementation and an opt-in HTTPS webhook implementation with SSRF/redirect/timeout limits
- [ ] Resolve opaque credential references only at send time and never store endpoints, tokens, or hostile transport prose in run/outbox payloads
- [ ] Supersede self-resolved alerts and preserve failed delivery as non-blocking Mission Control state

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Restart at every schedule/send boundary converges to one provider-visible idempotency key
- [ ] Unavailable and ambiguous receivers exhaust bounded retry without blocking or losing the canonical decision
- [ ] Batch, digest, quiet-hours, and escalation schedules survive restart under a fake clock
- [ ] Redirects, private-network endpoints, credential leaks, and cross-channel requests fail closed
