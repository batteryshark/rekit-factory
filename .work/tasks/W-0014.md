## Goal

## Requirements

- [ ] Concurrency, retry, and cost ceilings are explicit per run

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] A fixture fans out recon and analysis, then enqueues one evidence-driven follow-up without duplicating work
- [ ] Restarting during fan-out resumes unfinished workers exactly once
