## Goal
Select or implement the subsystem that produces source-bound campaign risk measurements without deriving risk from prose or notification policy.

## Requirements
- [ ] Define the authoritative risk producer and its bounded 0..100 semantics
- [ ] Bind every measurement to an exact checkpoint source revision and digest
- [ ] Call `CampaignController.record_risk_measurement` only after the producing fact is durably committed
- [ ] Preserve source continuity, replay safety, restart recovery, and terminal-state rejection
- [ ] Keep estimation evidence private while exposing only the canonical score/source identity

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Real campaign progress produces measurements without manual controller calls
- [ ] Replayed or regressed source facts cannot change the measurement stream
- [ ] Crossing the campaign contract threshold reaches the existing notification pipeline exactly once
- [ ] No model prose, heuristic fallback, or missing fact can fabricate a score
