## Goal
Make evidence blobs, evidence rows, audits, Factory events/artifacts, and Muster tool completion converge after interruption without inventing proof or duplicating publication.

## Requirements
- [ ] Enumerate and inject failure between every evidence blob, evidence-row, audit, event, artifact, and tool-completion boundary
- [ ] Define one idempotent reconciliation key that binds the authorized invocation, exact result bytes, evidence identity, and ledger work item
- [ ] Repair committed evidence that is missing its projection/event/completion while refusing missing or conflicting canonical bytes
- [ ] Preserve approval, scope, manifest, and invocation authority during every retry or repair

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Restart at every injected boundary converges to either no visible result or one fully linked evidence publication and one tool completion
- [ ] A validated/finding outcome never becomes visible before its required evidence and proof references
- [ ] Conflicting evidence or invocation identities fail closed with a bounded diagnostic

## Notes
This task owns evidence/tool publication only. It does not claim terminal exports, dossier filesystem publication, or run creation recovery.
