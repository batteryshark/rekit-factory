## Goal
Turn private typed ground truth and an evidence-cited typed submission into one objective, byte-deterministic score report without model grading or prose interpretation.

## Requirements
- [x] Fail closed on task/version/target/scoring-version/question-set drift, missing or extra answers, wrong answer kinds/options, and unknown forbidden-claim IDs
- [x] Award exact observed and reproducible-absent matches, score unknown as zero, and apply declared incorrect and forbidden-claim penalties
- [x] Clamp total score at zero, expose maximum and total penalty, and emit components/diagnostics in canonical question/claim order
- [x] Derive a stable score identity without timestamps, randomness, environment state, or model calls
- [x] Treat v1 dossier citations as structurally eligible only; do not claim semantic evidence sufficiency or current trust-anchor verification

## Acceptance Criteria
- [x] A correct positive and correct evidence-backed absence receive their exact weights while unknown receives zero
- [x] Wrong answers and asserted known forbidden behavior apply exact declared penalties without a negative total
- [x] Identity drift, malformed rubric values, missing/extra questions, and unknown forbidden IDs fail closed
- [x] Reordering set-like answers, dossiers, or citations preserves numeric components, totals, and diagnostics while the report remains bound to each exact canonical input digest
- [x] Unchanged canonical inputs produce byte-identical reports entirely offline

## Plan
Implement the pure scorer and focused in-memory fixtures in an isolated ReverseBench worktree. Packaging and persistence remain separate lanes until an integration owner combines them.

## Notes
No free-form model grading, evidence replay, current W-0025 anchor verification, baselines, aggregation, thresholds, promotion gates, or variance claims are in this slice.
