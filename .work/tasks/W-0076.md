## Goal
Let an operator select whether finding notifications begin at reproduced or accepted while preserving one exact proof-linked delivery.

## Requirements
- [ ] Add a server-declared finding-stage policy with `reproduced` and `accepted` values
- [ ] Bind configuration revisions and candidate identity to the selected stage
- [ ] Suppress reproduced delivery when accepted-only is selected without losing later acceptance
- [ ] Preserve campaign-owned dossier qualification, redaction, replay, and supersession behavior
- [ ] Expose the bounded selection in Mission Control without accepting notification prose or payloads

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] One finding crossing both stages emits exactly one notification at the configured stage
- [ ] Changing policy cannot replay an already delivered equivalent or strand an actionable transition
- [ ] The delivered deep link resolves to the exact qualified proof dossier when one exists
- [ ] Reconnect, restart, stale revision, and hostile browser fields fail closed
