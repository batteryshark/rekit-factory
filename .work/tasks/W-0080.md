## Goal
Define the immutable, content-addressed identity and provenance envelope that W-0029 can consume later without asserting that W-0028 has produced a successful or eligible evaluation.

## Requirements
- [x] Classify the proposed subject using the bounded W-0029 capability kinds, including non-promotable target-specific evidence
- [x] Bind exact capability content/version, scope, prerequisites, risks, originating runs, proof bundles, and opaque owner-qualified evaluation-result references
- [x] Give identical subjects a stable cross-campaign dedupe identity while retaining a separate hash for each exact evidence set
- [x] Reject extra fields, unsafe identifiers, malformed digests, duplicate references, noncanonical order, and forged identities
- [x] Carry no eligibility, score interpretation, approval, staging, installation, or publication state

## Acceptance Criteria
- [x] An exact record round-trips deterministically and cannot be mutated
- [x] Identical subjects from different runs share a subject identity without conflating their evidence records
- [x] A referenced ReverseBench result remains opaque and cannot introduce browser/model-authored scores or eligibility
- [x] Target-specific evidence is representable but receives no promotable/installable claim

## Progress Log
- 2026-07-14T04:30:00.000Z — Completed the dependency-safe contract slice. `PromotionCandidate` is an immutable canonical record with separate subject and evidence-set hashes, bounded exact owner-qualified references, strict reconstruction, and no evaluation or mutation authority. W-0029 remains gated by W-0028 for interpreting results, eligibility, staging, dual approval, install, rollback, and lifecycle persistence.
